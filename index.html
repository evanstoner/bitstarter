La Crêpe
Patrice at the order window. © Red Truck Eats

There's a wonderful little crêperie in Melbourne, FL called La Crêpe. It is owned by a Frenchman named Patrice and his wife Celeste. Patrice makes sweet and savory crêpes to order, while Celeste prepares a wide variety of bubble teas. I would very much like to enjoy another meal from La Crêpe before I leave the area.

As a poor college student, I don't have the resources to dine at La Crêpe. With your help, I'll be able to purchase one of Patrice's delicious savory crêpes, as well as a sweet boba tea from Celeste.

Two boba teas, one with pearls and one without. © Red Truck Eats

FAQ

$10.50...that's a nice round number.
Yes it is. A crêpe costs $7.00 and a boba tea costs $3.50. Tax? The price includes it, if you pay with cash. Otherwise, Patrice uses Square, which charges tax on top of the $10.50.
Why are all these pictures from Red Truck Eats?
Red Truck Eats is a nice little food blog written by two girls (Vanessa and Lucy) who drive around in an old red truck, trying food around the Space Coast. They've posted a wonderful entry about La Crêpe, which you should check out. I'm using their fantastic photos on my assignment page. (I hope they don't mind.)
